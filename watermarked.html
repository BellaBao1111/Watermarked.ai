Our AI-powered solution embeds imperceptible watermarks directly into your data (text, images, audio, and more) using advanced algorithms. These watermarks act as a silent defense:

Disruption

When watermarked data is used to train deepfake models, it actively disrupts the model's ability to generate convincing forgeries.

Even if a deepfake model bypasses disruption, it unintentionally replicates the watermark in its outputs. Our platform instantly identifies these markers, exposing synthetic content.
